Welcome to the digital Devil's Advocate! The Devil's Advocate is a scholastic organization that creates a newspaper that is written, edited, and published by the students of Columbia High School. This website houses an archive of articles that have or will be published in the Devil's Advocate newspaper. Articles can be found in their respective categories in the menubar at the top of the page, or by searching for them using the icon in the top-right corner. This website is unfinished and constantly being improved; if you find any bugs or have suggestions for features, please email Benson Haley at 21haleybe1@egcsd.org.

To participate in creating the newspaper, join the Devil's Advocate Google Classroom with the code uhea4cu. For further information, contact the club's coordinators: Mrs. Asenbauer (asenbauerme@egcsd.org) and Mrs. Macpherson (macphersonka@egcsd.org).
